About PixelCraft Pro

Learn about our mission, vision, and the team behind our powerful image processing tools

Our Story

PixelCraft Pro was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to make professional-grade image processing tools accessible to everyone. We believe that powerful editing capabilities should be available to designers, developers, and content creators without the need for expensive software or extensive technical knowledge.

Our Technology

We leverage cutting-edge AI and machine learning algorithms to deliver exceptional results. Our tools are built on a robust cloud infrastructure that ensures fast processing times and reliable performance, even with large files.

Our Team

We're a diverse team of engineers, designers, and AI specialists passionate about creating tools that empower creativity. With decades of combined experience in image processing and web technologies, we're committed to continuously improving our platform.

Our Values

  • Privacy First: We never store your images after processing
  • Accessibility: Tools designed for users of all skill levels
  • Innovation: Continuously improving with the latest technologies
  • Quality: Delivering professional-grade results every time

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the image compression work?

Our compression algorithm uses advanced techniques to reduce file size while maintaining visual quality. You can adjust the compression level based on your needs.

Is there a file size limit?

We support files up to 50MB for most tools. For premium users, this limit is increased to 200MB.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No. We process images in memory and automatically delete them immediately after processing. We never store your files.

What image formats are supported?

We support JPEG, PNG, WebP, GIF, and BMP formats for input. Output formats vary by tool.
