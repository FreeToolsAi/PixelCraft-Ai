Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about PixelCraft Pro

What file formats are supported?

PixelCraft Pro supports all major image formats including:

  • JPEG/JPG - Most common photo format
  • PNG - Best for images with transparency
  • WebP - Modern efficient format
  • GIF - For animated images (background remover doesn't support animation)
  • BMP - Uncompressed bitmap format

Maximum file size is 10MB for most tools, 5MB for background remover.

Is my data safe and private?

Absolutely! Your privacy is our top priority:

  • Most processing happens directly in your browser
  • Images are automatically deleted from our servers after processing
  • We never store, share, or analyze your personal images
  • All data transmission is encrypted with SSL/TLS
  • We comply with GDPR and other privacy regulations

Why is my image processing slow?

Processing speed depends on several factors:

  • Image size: Larger images take more time to process
  • Internet connection: Faster connections improve upload/download speeds
  • Device performance: More powerful devices process faster
  • Tool complexity: AI-powered tools like background removal take longer

Tips for faster processing:

  • Resize images before uploading if possible
  • Use a stable internet connection
  • Close other browser tabs to free up memory

Is PixelCraft Pro really free?

Yes! All our core tools are completely free to use:

  • No registration required
  • No hidden fees or subscriptions
  • No watermarks on processed images
  • Unlimited usage for personal and commercial projects

We're supported by ethical advertising and optional donations from users who love our tools.

Which browsers are supported?

PixelCraft Pro works on all modern browsers:

  • Chrome (recommended) - Version 80+
  • Firefox - Version 75+
  • Safari - Version 13+
  • Edge - Version 80+
  • Mobile browsers - iOS Safari, Chrome Mobile

For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of Chrome or Firefox.

How accurate is the background remover?

Our AI-powered background remover achieves high accuracy with:

  • Clear subjects: People, products, animals with good contrast
  • Simple backgrounds: Solid colors or minimal patterns work best
  • Good lighting: Well-lit images produce better results

Best practices for optimal results:

  • Use high-resolution images
  • Ensure good contrast between subject and background
  • Avoid busy or complex backgrounds when possible
  • Make sure the subject is clearly defined
